Courses

BCA

The term BCA stands for Bachelor of Computer Applications. A three-year undergraduate degree in technology and computer applications is known as a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). In this course, students will learn about a variety of computer applications as well as how to solve computer-related issues.

Duration:

BCA is a 3-year Bachelor degree comprising 6 Semesters

Eligibility Criteria:

The candidates seeking admission in BCA should have passed 10+2 or its equivalent examination in any stream through a recognized Board

Jobs after BCA

Technical Analyst,Data Scientist,Web Developer,Digital Marketer,Mobile Application Developer, Computer Programmer

Bsc(Computer Application)

B.Sc. Computer Applications is a 3-year full-time undergraduate degree program dealing with the study related to the various aspects of computer functioning. During this course study, the students can get in-depth knowledge on Windows, Programming Languages, Operating System, Web Designing and more.

Duration:

Bsc(Computer Application) is a 3-year Bachelors degree comprising 6 Semesters

Eligibility Criteria:

10+2 level in Science stream or Diploma or equivalent with 50% aggregate from a recognized board

Jobs after Bsc(Computer Application)

Mobile Application Developer, Computer Programmer, Computer Operator, Senior Applications Support Analyst, Computer Application Tutor etc.

BA(Computer Application)

B.A. in Computer Applications is an undergraduate course in information technology. Students who complete the program are expected to possess the skills and knowledge required to work in the field. The course includes the study of computer applications, computer science, programming, and software engineering

Duration:

BA(Computer Application) is a 3-year Bachelors degree comprising 6 Semesters

Eligibility Criteria:

The candidates seeking admission in BA(Computer Application) should have passed 10+2 or its equivalent examination in arts stream through a recognized Board

Jobs after BA(Computer Application)

the most common BA Computer Application jobs: Applications Developer, Cashier, Computer Operator, Office Assistant

MSC IT

A Master of Science in Information Technology is a master's degree in the field of information technology. MSc IT aims to provide theoretical as well as practical knowledge on topics like software development, data mining, computer systems, analytics etc

Duration:

Master of Science or MSc in information technology IT is a 2 years long post-graduate level master degree program

Eligibility Criteria:

The candidates willing to join the MSc IT program should possess a Bachelors degree in relevant disciplines like BSc in IT/ CS, BCA, BE/ BTech in IT or CS from a recognized university.They must also score a minimum of 50% percent marks in graduation to be eligible for the course.

Jobs after MSC IT

Software Developer, IT analyst, Maintenance Engineer, Application programmer etc

PGDCA

Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications (PGDCA) is a one year long professional post-graduate programme for candidates wanting to delve deeper into the world of computer application development. This programme is designed to provide higher level education in Information Technologies

Duration:

PGDCA is a one-year diploma course

Eligibility Criteria:

The basic PGDCA eligibility for the admission process is clearing the 10+2+6 or any Bachelor's degree from a recognised institution. The eligibility criteria demand a minimum of 50% marks from its aspirants on meritorious requirements

Jobs after PGDCA

After the successful completion of this program, one can work as: Software Engineer,Computer Programmer & Analyst,Interface Engineer, Java Developer, Project Manager, Information Security Analyst, IT Consultant
